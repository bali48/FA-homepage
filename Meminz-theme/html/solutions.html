Solutions

Fusion Advisor develops integrated products that provide maximum security and allow you to offer the most appropriate level of service to your clients.
Our products include:

Asset Portal

The Asset Portal provides branding capabilities, document management, and the ability to manage offline and held-away or hard-to-track assets. Primary client data will come from your existing portfolio management system or by direct custodial connections. Your clients will be able to have a live consolidated view of their assets between portfolio review sessions, and allow them to view their assets via your custom branded portal.

Client Vault

The Client Vault is our highly secure digital document storage facility. It is included in the Wealth Portal and Asset Portal, but you can choose to offer the Vault to your clients as a stand-alone service. The Client Vault is meant for communication with certain clients that may not warrant live account views or for third- party professionals that are sharing sensitive client information with you.

You can exchange confidential documents with your clients in a completely secure, professional and easy manner. With just a few clicks, you can transmit files of any size and make them available for download by your clients.

Easily publish advisor documents and reports including bulk-upload for performance reports to the Client Vault. Assist your client in organizing all their personal financial documents in our documents section for easy and secure access when they need it.

Wealth Portal 360°

The Wealth Portal 360° is the wealth management solution that addresses the broadest range of account aggregation and management needs, while helping to build a stronger brand for your business. Wealth Portal provides all the power and flexibility of enterprise-scale account aggregation solutions, in an affordable, easy-to-use, custom-branded package designed specifically for today's independent financial advisor.

Wealth Portal simplifies wealth management and extends the power of your existing financial systems by allowing you to:

  • Create a single view of all client accounts and assets from 1,000’s of online institutions
  • Monitor and manage offline and other hard-to-track assets
  • Archive and store investment-related documents and other important electronic files.
  • Build brand equity by allowing clients to view their portfolios via a customized Web-based portal with your own logo, template color themes, etc.

The Wealth Portal 360° will help establish your firm as a leader in wealth management

Let Fusion Advisor show you how to get complete, timely, accurate portfolio data right at your fingertips - how and when you need it - while reinforcing your brand!  You can deliver superior service to every client, providing greater impact to their bottom line and expanding their net worth.
